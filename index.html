What is Water ?.

Water is the primary and essential need of every living organism. It is the most precious gift by nature that helps in survival. 70% of our body is filled with water, which is why it is an essential part of our life. Water is needed for various purposes every day.It is a transparent, odorless, tasteless liquid, and a compound of hydrogen and oxygen.
